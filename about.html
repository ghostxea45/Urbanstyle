About UrbanStyle

UrbanStyle is a modern streetwear brand created with one clear idea in mind: fashion should be accessible for everyone. We believe that style is not something that should depend on how much money you have, but on how you express yourself. Our brand focuses on giving young people the confidence to wear what they like without worrying about expensive prices or luxury labels.

Our Story

UrbanStyle started as a small idea between creativity and frustration. Many students and young people love fashion, especially streetwear inspired by brands like Nike, Adidas, and Supreme, but most of these products are too expensive for everyday life. Because of that, we decided to build something different: a brand that keeps the same modern style but makes it affordable and realistic for students and everyday people.

At the beginning, UrbanStyle was just sketches, ideas, and mockups. We tested different designs, colors, and styles that represent urban culture, music, skateboarding, and daily street life. Over time, the idea grew into a full concept: a clothing brand that focuses on identity, comfort, and confidence.

Today, UrbanStyle represents more than clothing. It represents a lifestyle where people can feel comfortable being themselves, express their personality, and still look modern without spending too much money.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create affordable streetwear that still looks premium and modern. We want every customer to feel confident when they wear UrbanStyle, whether they are going to school, hanging out with friends, or just expressing their personal identity through fashion.

Our Vision

We aim to grow UrbanStyle into a recognized streetwear brand that connects with young people around the world. Our vision is to combine comfort, creativity, and urban culture into clothing that anyone can wear daily without feeling like they are spending too much money.

Our Values

  • Affordability without losing style
  • Confidence through fashion
  • Inspired by real street culture
  • Comfort and everyday wearability
  • Creativity and individuality

Founder

UrbanStyle was founded by Matias Toscano, a student who wanted to create something different from traditional fashion brands. He saw that many people had style ideas but not enough money to bring them to life, so he built this concept to solve that problem.

His goal is simple: make streetwear accessible for everyone without losing quality or identity.
